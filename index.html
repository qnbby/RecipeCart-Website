Recipe Cart

Description

RecipeCart is a complementary service to the existing technology, accommodating to user dietary needs in a seamless manner by providing a pipeline from the raw, physical ingredients to digitally manageable recipes. RecipeCart utilizes food object detection and classification, on top of barcode detection and process to identify the ingredients being presented and subsequently produces a list of user-relevant recipes.
